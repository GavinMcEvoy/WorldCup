Team Points = match points + stage points. Match points (win 1 / draw 0.5) are flat for everyone. Stage points are for the single furthest round a team reaches — weaker pots earn more for the same round (a Pot 4 surviving to the Round of 32 is worth 28 vs a Pot 1's 4), but the premium fades toward the final so no single underdog run runs away with the pool. No points until a team actually plays.
